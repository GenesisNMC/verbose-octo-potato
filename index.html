Who We Are

​CHANGE Lighthall badminton club is a Solihull based club. We are a competitive but friendly club currently with excess of 35 members. We welcome any player over the age of 16 of a decent standard. If you are looking for a suitable club, why not come along? Visits should be pre-arranged as we may not be able to accommodate visitors at all times.

Membership

Our membership fee is amongst the most competitive with 4 hours of court time a week. We play throughout the year. If you would like to try out our club, please contact Deven Odedra

When and Where

We meet on Monday nights at Langley School from 7.30- 10.00p.m. Also at Lode Heath School on Thursday nights from 8.00-10.00pm. We have four courts available in both halls and we use quality feather shuttles.

Langely Secondary School (Mondays) - Kineton Green Road, Solihull, B92 7ER

​Lode Heath School (Thursdays) - Lode Lane, Solihull, B91 2HW
